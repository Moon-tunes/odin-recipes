Lasagne

Description

This recipe creates the perfect lasagne guaranteed to warm any house during the dark winter months. Who doesn't like hot, molten cheese?

Ingredients

To make this delicious lasagne you will need:

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Mince
  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • White Sauce
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

Follow these steps if you would like to pursue pure joy:

  1. Chop the onions and garlic
  2. Cook the onions, garlic, mince and tomato sauce together
  3. Layer up the lasagne by starting with the mince mixture, then the lasagne sheets and finally the white sauce. Repeat until you run out of room
  4. Sprinkle on a generous amount of cheddar cheese and cook for 40 minutes at 180 degrees celsius
  5. Enjoy!
